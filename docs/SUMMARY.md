# MulmoCast: The AI-Native Platform Redefining How Ideas Are Shared

In an era where artificial intelligence is transforming every corner of the creative process, **MulmoCast** emerges as the first presentation platform built from the ground up for the AI age. Where legacy tools like PowerPoint and Keynote were designed for humans sitting at a keyboard, MulmoCast is designed for a world where AI and humans create together — and where video is the universal language of communication.

At the heart of MulmoCast is **MulmoScript** — a simple, structured script format that an AI model generates from a conversation, a document, or a URL. That single script drives the entire production pipeline: AI-generated voiceovers, automatically composed visuals, and a fully assembled video, rendered without a single cut or timeline edit. The result is a narrated, production-ready video in minutes — alongside any other format needed, from podcasts and slide decks to manga-style comics and PDFs.

Crucially, MulmoScript is designed to be read and edited by humans as naturally as by machines. Because the entire production is captured in a clean, structured format, creators can fine-tune any detail — adjusting narration, swapping visuals, or repurposing the same script for a completely different format, such as adapting a horizontal video into a vertical short for social media. This structured approach transforms content creation from a one-shot process into an iterative, flexible workflow where every output can be refined or reimagined with minimal effort.

The implications for video creation are profound. A researcher can turn a technical paper into an engaging narrated video. A startup can transform a business pitch into a cinematic presentation, complete with Ghibli-style animation, in multiple languages. An educator can convert lecture notes into an illustrated video series. MulmoCast handles every layer of video production — voice synthesis, image generation, subtitle rendering, and final assembly — while the creator stays focused on the message.

What sets MulmoCast apart is its unmatched breadth of AI model support. For video generation alone, creators can choose from Google's Veo, ByteDance's Seedance, Kling, MinMax/Hailuo, and PixVerse — each with its own distinct visual strengths — all from a single unified workflow. MulmoCast also integrates lip-sync models, bringing characters to life with natural, speech-synchronized motion. And through its image reference system, creators can define a character once and maintain visual consistency across every scene — something that has historically been one of the hardest challenges in AI video production. The same openness extends to image generation, voice synthesis, and language models, drawing on providers including OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, and ElevenLabs. No other platform gives creators this level of choice, ensuring that MulmoCast stays at the cutting edge as the AI landscape continues to evolve rapidly.

Fully open-source and backed by an active developer community with more than 160 releases since launch, MulmoCast is not just a new tool — it is a new foundation for video communication, purpose-built for a world where the best ideas deserve to reach every audience, in every format, on every screen.
